About Banas Marine

Banas Marine provides comprehensive marine services including vessel repair, ship maintenance, container handling, and logistics solutions. The company serves both local and international clients, ensuring reliability, safety, and efficiency across all marine operations. With a skilled technical team and modern facilities, Banas Marine delivers high-quality services that meet global maritime standards.

Our Mission

To deliver dependable and cost-effective marine, shipping, and logistics solutions through innovation, skilled expertise, and a commitment to customer satisfaction while maintaining safety and environmental responsibility.

Health, Safety & Environment

Banas Marine is committed to maintaining the highest standards of health, safety, and environmental protection. All operations are carried out following strict safety protocols to prevent accidents, protect personnel, and minimize environmental impact. The company promotes a culture of continuous improvement, ensuring a safe and sustainable working environment for all employees and partners.
